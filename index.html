About Career Compass 🧭

Feeling lost about which career path to take? Don’t stress — Career Compass is here to guide you! 🗣️

Hosted by Vaishnavi, this podcast is designed to help youth across India explore a wide range of career options. Through candid interviews with professionals from diverse fields, we uncover real stories — how they got started, the pros and cons of their journey, and the steps you can take to follow in their footsteps.

Whether you're in school, college, or just unsure of your next move, Career Compass offers inspiration, clarity, and practical advice to help you find your path and shape your future.
